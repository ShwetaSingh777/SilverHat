Project Overview

Enigma, a term denoting a perplexing situation, finds relevance in today’s world where understanding social media remains a challenging endeavor. Amidst the myriad problems and negative associations surrounding it, social media continues to be ubiquitous pass time. However, the question arises: “Why do people often focus solely on the detrimental aspects?” The distinguishing features of Enigma font serve as a testament to the positive impact one can have on social media platforms.

SOFTWARE USED

Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe After Effects
FontForge

Process

The journey to create my font began with exploring the hidden aspects of social media. Through brainstorming and moodboarding, I found a captivating theme that aligned with my vision. Translating ideas from sketches to digital using Adobe Illustrator, I refined the design with meticulous attention. Font Forge, the trusted font-making software, brought my artwork to life. Finally, testing the final product was an exhilarating moment, as my efforts materialized into a beautifully crafted font ready to make an impact.

Type Guidelines

THEME- Colorless beauty (Forgetting about the colors such as social media desperation, let’s focus on its emotional beauty.)
TONE OF VOICE- Bold, Chic, Elegant, Individualistic
STYLISH DESIGN- Empowers individuals to confidently express their true selves with a touch of glamour.
LARGE X-HEIGHT- Conveys a sense of pride
VARYING STROKE WIDTHS- Highlights uniqueness
HOOK AT THE FOOT- Social media hooks everyone
KERNING- Reducing space between letters to show unity in diversity.
