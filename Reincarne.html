Project Overview

Grandiose, an avante-garde luxury fashion brand, is poised to revolutionize the industry with its forthcoming collection, an extraordinary fusion of sustainability and artistic ingenuity. Breaking free from the convention, they have ingeniously repurposed surplus fabric from their opulent garments, breathing new life into each thread.

SOFTWARE USED

Origami
Microsoft Bing

Process

Through an extensive exploration of various mediums, including revisiting my tried-and-true processes, multiple name changes, haiku creation, ukiyo-e art study, and experimentation with Microsoft Bing AI art, I found myself unsatisfied with the results. However, undeterred, I took matters into my own hands, embracing a hands-on approach. Reincarne, our collection, reverently embodies the profound principles of Wabi Sabi. As the creative visionary overseeing packaging design, I fearlessly delved into an audacious quest for form and function. Employing Origami techniques, I painstakingly crafted the packaging, while honing my photography skills to achieve studio-like professionalism from the comforts of home.

Brand Guidelines

THEME- Formless paper (All the things are formless unless put together and given a form.)
TONE OF VOICE- Connected, Organic, Vibrant, Strong
LOGO- Peacock signifies the concepts of resurrection and rebirth in Japanese culture.
TYPE- An elegant handwriting full of imperfections and beauty
Color Palette- I used yellow and green as the primary colors and used some printed papers to make a few assets. Old magazine pages and waste paper can be used to make such assets.
