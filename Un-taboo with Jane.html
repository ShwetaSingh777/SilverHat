Project Overview

The brief from D&AD New Bloods 2023, presented by The Case for Her and Hey Jane, immediately captured my attention. As an active member of Girltelligence, I have developed a heightened awareness and advocacy for women's health and choice. The essence of the brief called for the creation of a campaign that not only reframes abortion as an essential right and routine procedure but also integrates it into the broader context of women's health.

SOFTWARE USED

Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe After Effects

Process

I enthusiastically embraced the "Un-taboo with Jane" brief and sought guidance from Julia, a senior copywriter at Publicis Poke and my previous D&AD New Bloods mentor. Extensive research and brainstorming led me to explore different ideas, but I felt unsatisfied. With Julia's support, I immersed myself in funny videos and memes, eventually discovering an idea I truly liked. I created a final video explaining my concept. I would appreciate your feedback after watching it.

Campaign Guidelines

CHALLENGE- Being unmarried and pregnant is the biggest taboo for women in India. And for her to get an abortion is even more difficult and embarrassing. The stigma leads to increase in maternal deaths due to abortion in India.
INSIGHT- People feel taboo sharing abortion content. But there is no taboo for sharing comic strips.
IDEA- Educating people through abortion comic strips that can be used as stickers too.
EXECUTION- Anyone who can get pregnant, uses pads. So, Hey Jane and The Case For Her collaborates with Indian pad company Whisper to supply comic strips. People read it, laugh at it and share it. We will also have a QR code attached that will take you to the main site with more comic stripes and abortion information.
