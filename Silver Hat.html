My Story

In my life, I have struggled through a lot of insecurities and identity crises. All those incidents made me realize how important it is to find your magic. I have come back with my silver hat to help people unveil their authentic magical selves to the world through branding and illustration. I would be taking risks, experimenting every day, trusting my instincts, and be a feminist. After a lot of brainstorming, I chose Silver Hat as my partner in design as silver is a color of elegance, mysteriousness, magic, and dreams. It is a color of feminine charm for me.

Process

In the coming years, I'm embracing new challenges and learning Cinema 4D for captivating 3D animations. Though tough at first, it's becoming easier and more exciting. Crafting a logo that merges an ampersand with a hat was a rewarding journey of sketches and determination, resulting in the perfect representation of my vision. I look forward to further growth and creative exploration in this realm. I have also honed my strategizing and coding skills to make this portfolio come to life.

Brand Guidelines

Theme- My motive is to help you find your magic, so the entire branding is set on the concept of "hidden magic".
Logo- It is an ampersand silver hat representing me uniting with client, elegance, and high-quality work.
Typography- I chose the contrasting fonts Playfair Display (transitional serif) and Futura (modern sans serif) as I will be a part of the brand's transitional phase to make them exist in this contemporary world.
Tone of voice- Elegant, mysterious, magical, and dreamy
